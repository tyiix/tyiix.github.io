The Mountains, more commonly known as the Godlands, is located around the center of the map in a realm. The terrain is a gray, mountainous area or a snowy area.

The Gods

White Demon

The White Demon follows the closest player and shoots out a spread shot of 3 Armor Piercing (Ignores your defense) shots that deals a fixed 45 damage. Do a mixed combination of strafing and circling to dodge its shots.

The White Demon has a chance of dropping Attack Potions and the Abyss of Demons dungeon, which is a source of Vitality Potions and sometimes Defense Potions.


Medusa

The Medusa is a dangerous god you should look out for. She chases the closest player and shoots out a spread of 5 green laser shots that do 100 damage each and occasionally throws out very high damaging grenades . It is especially dangerous when she is alongside other gods.
The best way to dodge the Medusa is to circle around her. Watch out for the grenades, they have killed many players.

The Medusa has a chance of dropping Speed Potions and the Snake Pit dungeon, which is a source of Speed Potions.


Ent God

The Ent God chases the closest player and shoots a spread of 5 fast shots that does 70 damage each. If you have high defense, it wont do that much damage. The shots are aimed at where you are going to go. Strafing left and right will trick the shots and is the best way to dodge his shots.

The Ent God has a chance of dropping Defense Potions and a really low chance of dropping the Magic Woods dungeon, which is a source of Speed Potions and Dexterity Potions.


Beholder

The Beholder closest player and shoots 5 white shots outwards in the shape of a star that deals 120 damage each and occasionally shoots a purple star that gives the blind status. The white shots are shot at the player's current movement while the purple star predicts movement.
The white shots are slow but they are dangerous. Since it is being shot in a star formation, there is plenty of space to dodge. Blind is a dangerous status effect in that it darkens your screen way more and you will have a hard time seeing things, unless you really brighten your screen.

The Beholder has a chance of dropping Defense Potions and a really low chance of dropping the Cursed Library dungeon, which is a source of Vitality Potions, Wisdom Potions and Attack Potions.

Lucky Gods

Lucky gods are golden, stronger versions of the standard gods. They all have similar behavior, with a few minor tweaks.

All Lucky Gods drop the same potions and dungeons as their normal counterparts, but with a guaranteed rate. They spawn with a 1% chance in place of a normal god.


Lucky White Demon

The Lucky White Demon follows the closest player and shoots out a spread of 5 Armor Piercing (Ignores your defense) shots that deals a fixed 60 damage. It randomly chooses between four patterns when shooting.

  • Normal spread, like the regular White Demon
  • Slow spread, as above but barely moves
  • Spike-shaped, with the center bullet moving the fastest
  • V-shaped, with the center bullet moving the slowest


Lucky Medusa

The Lucky Medusa chases the closest player while firing spreads of five bolts, with the outer four having a curved pattern. She occasionally throws out golden bombs. These bombs have a small radius, but they deal 175 damage and petrify on contact, making you unable to move or shoot for two seconds.


Lucky Ent God

The Lucky Ent God chases the closest player while firing 100 damage bolts. These bolts can hit multiple players and they predict where you will be, just like normal Ent Gods. Bursts are randomly chosen between fast (same speed as Ent God shots) and slow (half the speed).


Lucky Beholder

The Lucky Beholder slowly orbits the nearest player at a distance of 5-6 tiles while shooting shotguns of slow-moving bullets. Each burst releases two shotguns, one aimed toward the player, and one aimed directly away.
The more dangerous part is this white star because, while it does not deal damage, it inflicts Darkness for 3 seconds. Darkness reduces visibility to a small area around your character and causes enemies outside this range to vanish. They can still see you, and other players will see them shooting, but they cannot hurt you until they get close.
